A Vision for Impact

The Sales Masterclass For Business Owners (SMBO) is a focused, high-impact event hosted by Dr. Stephen Akintayo — Chairman, SACI Holdings — designed for business owners who are serious about building profitable businesses, increasing deal flow, and selling with integrity.

This is a practical learning experience drawn from years of building, selling, negotiating, and scaling across real estate, consulting, and enterprise.

Many businesses struggle not because the product is poor, but because the sales process is weak.

Why This Masterclass Matters

Addressing the core challenges of modern business growth.

Low Conversions

Leads are coming in, but they aren't translating into revenue. Your funnel is leaking, and it's time to plug the holes.

Failed Closures

Deals start strong with initial interest but fail to close. Learn how to maintain momentum until the commitment.

Pricing Hesitation

Pricing feels uncomfortable to defend. Gain the confidence to position your value correctly without discounting.

Inconsistent Growth

Sales feels forced instead of natural. Create a predictable engine that drives results day after day.

What You Will Learn

Everything taught will be practical, structured, and applicable immediately. We break down the complex into actionable steps.

  • How to sell without sounding desperate or aggressive
  • The mindset shifts that separate top closers from average sellers
  • How to structure conversations that lead naturally to decisions
  • Pricing confidence and value positioning
  • Building trust before asking for commitment
  • Creating repeat buyers and long-term relationships
  • Why most sales efforts fail and how to correct them

Who This Is For?

This masterclass is intentionally not for everyone. It is for those building to last.

Business Owners

Entrepreneurs scaling their vision.

Sales Professionals

Consultants and high-level closers.

Real Estate Practitioners

Agents and investors in high-ticket markets.

Founders

Leaders who want predictable revenue.

If you are building something that must scale and last, this room is for you.
